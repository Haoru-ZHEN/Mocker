Setup your Authenticator

FYP_Authenticator is developed to secure the authentication process by implementing 2FA, avoiding unauthorized actors from accessing the applications.

Steps:

1. Register an account in FYP_Authenticator
2. Click the "+" button
3. Scan QR code or enter the key manually
4. Click the "Connect" button
5. Use the OTP to access Mocker
